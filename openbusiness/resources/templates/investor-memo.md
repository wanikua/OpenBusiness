description = "Frames the report as first-pass diligence for investors and strategy teams."
+++

Emphasize:

- investability, market structure, business quality, scalability, and downside risk
- customer proof, willingness to pay, retention/expansion clues, and margin structure
- funding stage, investors, valuation, ownership, and likely capital needs
- the few assumptions that would most change the investment conclusion

Avoid:

- generic praise
- pretending that missing private-company metrics are known
- replacing evidence labels with a single confidence score
