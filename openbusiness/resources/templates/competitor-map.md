description = "Frames the report around alternatives, competitive response, and market positioning."
+++

Emphasize:

- direct competitors, indirect substitutes, incumbent platforms, and manual workarounds
- where the company wins by product, channel, trust, price, ecosystem, or timing
- likely competitor responses and which response would hurt most
- which customer segment is least defensible and which segment is most attractive

Do not turn competitor lists into unsupported certainty. Mark unknown market-share,
win-rate, churn, or pricing claims as `[MISSING]` unless a source supports them.
