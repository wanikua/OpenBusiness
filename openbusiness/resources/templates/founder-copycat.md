description = "Frames the report for founders studying what to copy, avoid, or attack."
+++

Emphasize:

- what is worth copying: customer segment, wedge, channel, pricing, product shape, or trust mechanism
- what is dangerous to copy without the same assets, timing, capital, or distribution
- what a new entrant could attack, ignore, or re-bundle
- the fatal weakness that would matter most to a focused startup

Keep evidence labels visible. If a tactic is attractive but unsupported by sources,
mark it `[INFERRED]`; if the required validation data is absent, mark it `[MISSING]`.
