Use the normal OpenBusiness report structure. Keep the report dense, evidence-labeled,
and suitable for founders, operators, investors, and strategy teams.

Do not weaken the evidence-label contract. Every material claim must remain one of:
`[VERIFIED:url]`, `[INFERRED]`, or `[MISSING]`.
